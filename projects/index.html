Big Data Buses

Android: Ankit Shah Riyu Banerjee

IOS: Vaish Raman Ayush Mehra Varun Rana

Backend: Russell Trupiano Victor Fan

Our mission is to significantly improve the bus experience at University of Michigan. We hope to collect statistics and rider data relating to bus usage to create a reliable representation of real-time transportation services. We will then relay this information through phone and web applications. We intend for this application to be used by the population of Ann Arbor residents who use the Blue Bus service on a regular basis.

UMich Cosign APIs for Android/iOS

Omkar Moghe, Ameya Khare, Nick Lamping, Charles Wang

A native Android and iOS login API for The University of Michigan cosign authentication interface. Our goal is to handle the cosign login cookies and expiration allowing developers to easily create applications for UMich services without having to worry about login and authentication issues.
